Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?
Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases in the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID that was used for the original purchase. If the restore doesn't work, please contact our payment support.
What's the difference between SwiftQuantum and QuantumNative?
SwiftQuantum is a Swift framework for iOS/macOS developers to integrate quantum computing into their apps. QuantumNative is an educational iOS app that helps users learn quantum computing through interactive lessons and simulations.
How can I access real IBM Quantum hardware?
Real IBM QPU access is available through our Enterprise subscription ($29.99/month) in QuantumNative. For SwiftQuantum framework users, you can use the QuantumBridge API to access IBM Quantum hardware programmatically.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find QuantumNative, and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
Is my quantum circuit data private?
Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your quantum circuits and simulation data are stored securely and encrypted. When using IBM Quantum hardware, only anonymous circuit data is sent for execution. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
