## Tab 1: Lab (Laboratory)

### What is it?
The Lab is your personal quantum physics laboratory! You can play with quantum bits (qubits) and see how they behave.

### Main Features

| Feature | What it does | Why it's cool |
|---------|--------------|---------------|
| **Bloch Sphere** | Shows where a qubit "points" in 3D | See quantum states as a ball! |
| **Gate Controls** | Apply quantum operations | Like pressing buttons to change the qubit |
| **Measurement** | Collapse the quantum state | Find out if it's 0 or 1 |
| **Phase Display** | Shows rotation angles | Understand quantum phases |

### Simple Explanation

Imagine a coin that's both heads AND tails at the same time - that's a qubit in superposition!

- **Hadamard Gate (H)**: Makes the coin spin so it's both heads and tails
- **Pauli-X Gate**: Flips the coin from heads to tails
- **Pauli-Z Gate**: Rotates the coin but keeps it in the same up/down state
- **Measure**: Stop the coin and see what you got!

### What You'll Learn
- How qubits work
- What superposition really means
- Why quantum computers are different from regular computers

| Category | What it includes | Difficulty |
|----------|-----------------|------------|

| **Entanglement** | Bell State, GHZ State | Beginner to Intermediate |

### Featured Circuits Explained

| Circuit | What it does | Like a... |

### How to Use

1. **Browse** - Look through circuit categories

### Simple Explanation

Think of circuits like:

- **Shots** = How many times we repeat the experiment

## Glossary

| Term | Simple Meaning |
|------|----------------|
| **Qubit** | Quantum bit - can be 0, 1, or both! |
| **Superposition** | Being multiple things at once |
| **Entanglement** | Two qubits that are magically connected |
| **Gate** | An operation that changes a qubit |
| **Measurement** | Looking at a qubit (makes it choose 0 or 1) |
| **QASM** | Code language for quantum circuits |
| **QPU** | Quantum Processing Unit (the real quantum computer) |
